GUIDELINES

Arma 3 Exile is a game that takes players into a world that combines military tactics, stealth and, game play of prior mods before it with the Building and Trading merged in as well.
 
With this in mind, being such a vast and complex array of Gameplay some Rules and Guidelines have been set and we require each and every player to READ these in order to play on the Server.
 
(NOTE: These rules are subject to periodical changes due to the constant 'loopholes" that present themselves, so please check back occasionally for updates. Feel free to send us feedback, we value it!)
 

COMBAT LOGGING

Combat logging means that when you engage in combat and then log off in anyway and loose connection from the server.
You are in combat only if you shoot, someone near you shoots, you ram your vehicle, or someone rams a vehicle on you - Accidentally or on purpose.
If you witness a combat log you should report it in the Report a Player section of the forums. Video proof is the best when it comes to these reports.
Excessive Combat Logging will Result in a timeout or a temp ban.

SAFE ZONES

Safe zones have been set up in order for players to conduct their TRADING under a safe non-combat environment, and we REQUIRE players to conduct themselves in a mature and timely fashion: e.g. conduct your business and move on, basically. Players may wait for a "pick up" in these areas and are to conduct themselves accordingly whilst doing so.
 
The below mentioned are Rules & Guidelines when in these areas:
 
(ADMINS discretion also applies to the TRADE areas and is to be adhered to as if a rule inside the Trade zones and we won't tolerate GRIEFING in any way, shape or form!)
 
  • You may not Hook/Lift Locked Vehcles inside the trader, if caught you will be slain and given a 24 hour break from the server. Repeated offenses will result may result in a permanant ban from the server.
  • You may not kill anyone inside a safe zone (God mode script should prevent this).
  • Vehicles must be parked in parking spots at the terminal trader or they risk being moved/deleted.
  • We are not responsable for vehicles left at the traders.
  • We will not reinburse for vehicles/gear lost due to vehicles being left at the traders.
  • Exploiting the safe zone/God mode is not allowed. This includes, but is not limited to: Repeatedly going into and out of the safezone to enter/exit combat.
  • No ramming in safe zone.
 
YOU ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR YOURSELF
Killing people around safe zones and camping is discouraged but allowed ( This does not include the above rule baring using the safezone to enter/exit combat)
 
  • Bandits will be roaming around killing and stealing Your Valuables.
  • Cars can currently be stolen, this is not against the rules, Use caution to keep your vehicles safe from thiefs.
  • Reinbursment for stolen vehicles is solely at the discression of the Admin and should not be expected.
  • Server Provides you with a God Mode Area so no one can kill you. So be careful when getting in/out of safe zones.

BASES

Territory Flags
Territory Flags are required for base building. Territory Flags currently cannot be stolen. This may change with future updates to Exile.
Other player curently cannot build on a Territory that is not theirs. This may change in future updates of Exile.
There is currently no limit to the number of items used in the construction of your base. This may change in the future.
 
All bases are currently destructable. Be wary of raids as you choose your base location.
Doors can be cracked currently, while the difficulty of this is sizable, you are encouraged to build your base in such a way to account for this.
There is currently no maintinance for bases. This may change in future updates of Exile.
 
No Skybases
Bases that are floating without having any contact with the ground will be deleted. Bases must be supported by the ground or a building.
 
Base Takeover
Bases currently cannot be taken over. this may change with future updates of Exile.
 
Base Kamikaze
Using Kamikaze maneuvers to kamikaze a base or Safe Zone Trader IS AGAINST RULES.
 
Glitching and Exploiting
We do not allow any form of glitching or exploiting on our servers. Duping is the same as glitching and exploiting. Players caught glitching or exploiting will be removed from our servers. There are too many glitches and exploits to be fleshed out here, however, that does not mean just because it is not said in the rules that it isn't a glitch and/or exploit. A glitch is ANYTHING that SHOULD NOT happen but does.
Glitching or exploiting into bases or any normally inaccessible areas in any way, shape, or form is against rules.
 
 
BUILDING AREAS
  • You cannot block off Military loot spawns. This includes any green military tower/barracks. (Full list of buildings will be poste at a future date. Left to admin discretion until list is posted.)
  • You may not build anywhere within 1000m of trader or trader cities. This includes safes and any other types of lockables. If even one corner of your base is less than 1000m away from the EDGE of the trader or safe zone, you are breaking the rules.
  • You may not block any major roads, or have any building components touching the paved road. An Tempest should easily be able to fit underneath any overpasses you build. Building on small dirt roads is fine.
  • The blocking off of entrances to barracks to funnel players across into a certain side is not acceptable either; it's unsportsmanlike and is a form of barricading.
ADMINS WILL REMOVE BASES/ITEMS THAT ARE NOT ABIDING BY THE RULES
 
 
 

Side Chat and Personal behavior

DO NOT
  • Use voice in Side Chat. We have a public TeamSpeak server for players that are interested in using voice chat while they game. We have public channels for each server. Players are allowed to create a temporary channel that has a password if they so wish. The channel will delete itself as soon as the last person leaves. We have donator channels as well that are password protected and stay on the TeamSpeak server regardless of whether or not someone is within the channel.
  • Ghost in TeamSpeak. If caught, you can be removed from TeamSpeak.
  • Use any kind of Racist, Homophobic, or Discriminatory comments. It will not be tolerated, even if the remark is made towards a friend or in a joking matter. This includes comments regarding sex trafficking, pedophilia, necrophilia, and or any other touchy subject matter of the like.
  • Using Slang will get your kicked automatically from the server.
  • Be overly abusive towards another player. Shit talk happens; it is part of the game. Know when to call it quits.
 
Topics such as politics, religion, and world events are bound to come up in side chat. These topics are very touchy and people can get worked up over them very easily. We suggest leaving such things out of side chat, however, talking about them is not against rules. Please take the discussion to another location besides side chat if you are asked to do so, even if it is by another player. We offer temporary TeamSpeak channels that can be password protected. These channels can be used as voice communication and they also have the ability to transfer text to ONLY the people in the channel via the chat box of TeamSpeak.
